COVID-19 Announcement
What are we doing to protect our employees?
  • We are providing employees with Sanitation Stations throughout their work environment.
  • We are providing masks for employees to wear while working.
  • We are sanitizing all work areas throughout the night.
  • We are practicing social distancing with our staff as much as possible.
  • Every employee is required to answer a series of questions provided by the CDC and have their temperature checked prior to entering the facility. If they answer yes to any of these questions or have a temperature of 100.4 degrees or higher they will not be allowed to work.
What can guests do to protect themselves?
  • If possible, please take the temperatures of everyone in your party before arriving at the facility to make sure they are below 100.4 degrees.
  • We are recommending all guests to wear face masks when at the haunt.
  • Please use the hand sanitizing stations throughout the facility as needed.
  • Please be aware of markers and the parties in front of and around you to maintain social distancing when at the haunt and in the parking lot.
  • It is your responsibility to maintain social distancing while visiting the haunt.
COVID – 19 WARNING

We have taken enhanced health and safety measures for you, our other Guests and Cast Members. You must follow all posted instructions while visiting Meyers Creek Haunted Woods.

An inherent risk of exposure to COVID-19 exists in any public place where people are present. COVID-19 is an extremely contagious disease that can lead to severe illness and death. According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, senior citizens and guest with underlying medical conditions are especially vulnerable.

By visiting Meyers Creek Haunted Woods, you voluntarily assume all risks related to exposure to COVID-19.
